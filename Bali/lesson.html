Lesson — Was & Were

Detailed explanation, examples, dialog, vocabulary

1. Grammar: When to use was and were

Was is used with I / he / she / it. Were is used with you / we / they.

PositiveI was at the market.
NegativeShe was not (wasn't) at school.
QuestionWere they at the playground?

2. Examples

  • He was at the beach yesterday.
  • They were in the classroom last Monday.
  • We were at the market last Sunday.

3. Reading: Dialog — In Bali

Made’s Holiday in Bali
Made and his family went to Bali last weekend. They flew from Makassar on Friday and arrived in Bali at night. They stayed in a hotel near the beach. The hotel had a big swimming pool. Made was very happy.

On Saturday morning, the weather was sunny. Made, his parents, and his little brother went to the beach. They played in the sand, swam in the sea, and drank fresh coconut water. Made tried surfing for the first time. He fell many times, but he laughed and had fun. His father took many pictures. At night, they ate dinner at a seafood restaurant. Made liked the grilled shrimp and fish soup. After dinner, they walked around the art market and bought souvenirs like bracelets and T-shirts.

On Sunday, Made and his father stayed at the hotel to rest. His mother went to the traditional market to buy fruits and spices. Made’s little brother went with her. He liked seeing all the colorful things in the market.

Before going home, they visited Tanah Lot Temple and watched the sunset. It was very beautiful. Made felt happy because he had a fun holiday with his family.

Questions: 🧠 Latihan Soal “Was” dan “Were”
- Where was Made last weekend?
- What was the weather like on Saturday?
- Who was at the market on Sunday?
- Was Made happy during the holiday?
- Was the hotel near the beach?
- Where were Made and his family on Saturday morning?
- What were they doing at the beach?
- Were Made’s parents with him at the beach?
- Were the souvenirs from the art market beautiful?
- Were Made and his father at the hotel on Sunday?

4. Vocabulary (Kamus)

beach — pantai
market — pasar
playground — taman bermain
kitchen — dapur
